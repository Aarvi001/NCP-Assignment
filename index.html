ABOUT US


Is winter even winter if you’ve not had at least one bowl full of halwa? An integral part of the Indian dessert family, it is prepared primarily in the winters because carrots are a winter vegetable. Many, however, have to abstain from it because of the high number of calories it contains.

In our opinion, no one should keep themselves away from this sweet goodness. Hence, we’ve brought you varieties of halwa which are low on the calorie but scores big time in the taste department. So, enjoy the beloved dessert without compromising on taste or health!
